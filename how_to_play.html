How to play

"This is a mystery game in which you are looking for your friends who went missing over the weekend. particularly your girlfriend and her sister. They are all you friends but you are most concerned with the two afformentioned people. There was a storm in the mountain your girlfriend along with your best friend and others stayed at the cabin and you could not get in contact due to the storm since the day before.
There are clues scattered about that paint a story of what happened in the mountain. choose where you go next based on the clues you find throughout your journey. Some clues lead to trouble if you make the wrong choice you may end up in a pit, or eaten by the wendigo's that come out at night until dawn.
