How to play

"This is a mystery game in which you are looking for your friend who went missing over the weekend. There was a storm in the cabin in the mountains she and her friends stayed at and you could not get in contact with her since the day before. There are clues scattered about that paint a story of what happened in the mountain. choose where you go next based on the clues you find throughout your journey. Some clues lead to trouble if you make the wrong choice you may end up in a pit, or eaten by the wendigo's that come out at night until dawn.
